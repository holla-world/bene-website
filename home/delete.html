Delete
Should you affirm your wish to delete your account, kindly forward an email to support@bene.world, ensuring it contains both your unique user ID and nickname.
Upon initiating the account deletion request, please note that a 30-day grace period will commence before permanent deletion. Within this timeframe, your account will be placed in a deactivated state, rendering it inaccessible. You retain the flexibility to rescind the deletion request at any point during this 30-day window. Failure to do so will result in the irreversible erasure of your account on the 30th day following the initial request submission.
